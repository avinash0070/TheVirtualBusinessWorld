About us

The Virtual Business World is devoted to enhancing networking, through organizing Virtual Webinars, Seminars, Exhibitions, Networking meets, with the best possible combination of Humans &Technology. TVBW covers almost all the Industries, precisely from rubber to food, and everything you can think of. Our USP is our virtual networking methodology as our networking meets are planned to happen in more than 100 languages, tagging participants across the globe. We are an independent, small, solely owned firm with no physical presence as we are 100% virtual and paperless. We at TVBW feel proud in sharing the fact that though being new to the Industry, We all are geared up to represent more than a dozen reputed trade exhibitions, conferences, and webinars globally as a trade agency. Our full-time employees serve the Industry with a strong concept of virtual working based at different geographical locations across the world. Our financials even including the invoices sent to you, are run through ERP grade Software which is a cloud-based web application. Our business meetings either internal or external are all virtual on different meeting platforms. So the next time you would love to see us make sure that you have got zoom installed. We would love to see you too.

Why virtual

Being virtual provides you a unique ability to be present at multiple different locations while just sitting in one place. It saves your time, money, and helps you in taking instant business decisions though your client might be sitting a thousand miles away. Though the pandemic of 2020 has really taught us multiple lessons of why being virtual, however, our vision differs from all, because our utmost priority has always been connecting those for whom networking is a hefty expense and time-consuming.

F.A.Q

  • How to register for a specific webinar?

    All our events are strictly by invitation only, however, it's mandatory for you to fill-up the form. Once forms are received our team will review the application and will share the invite within a span of 72 hours before the event. We work on the zero-fee model in regard to participation as a delegate

  • How to become a sponsor for a particular event?

    We would love to see you as a sponsor for our event however even for being a sponsor in a particular event, you need to complete the registration process, once registration is complete we would certainly get back to you basis availability. However, to discuss more you can get back to us on the handphone numbers or email address mentioned on our website.

  • How to make the payments?

    We strongly discourage payments through plastic or paper. For the safety of our customers, our bank details are mentioned on our webpage. You can simply make NEFT/IMPS/RTGS on our bank account details.

  • How will I receive the confirmation about our payments made?

    Once the payment is received, you are requested to contact us via email with the details of the transaction in order to confirm and share the payment receipt.

  • Can I become a sponsor for multiple events and are there any discounts available?

    Yes, you can, but unfortunately, we strictly adhere to the policy of NO discounts in any scenario.

  • Do we get media coverage to be a sponsor?

    Depends on the event to event.
